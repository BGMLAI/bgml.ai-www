What BGML.ai is

Not decentralized AI as a slogan. A falsifiable wedge plus the protocol to scale it.

The current AI economy compounds capital: capital buys compute, compute buys better AI, and better AI compounds capital. Centralization breaks when local knowledge dominates and failures correlate; AI inference has both. BGML.ai moves value back to the edge by making idle hardware useful infrastructure.

1

Complete local models

Each device runs a model it can actually support. Phones, laptops, desktops, and VPS nodes participate at different tiers instead of pretending to be one giant GPU.

2

Cache-first swarm inference

Queries hit local semantic cache first, then route across devices by task type, model fit, health, latency, reputation, and historical superadditivity.

3

Shared human knowledge

Non-private, high-quality AI outputs are cached, scored, deduplicated, and added to common memory. Private data stays local; public knowledge compounds.

4

Federated orchestration

The current hub exists for auditability. The target is peer discovery, federated routing, encrypted pheromone traces, and no central owner of coordination.

Trust layer

Reputation, negative cache, and pheromone encryption instead of blind consensus.

Social media became a forge of falsehood: optimized feeds mix rumor, status, fear, and repetition until people lose signal. BGML.ai goes the other way: heterogenization through many local contexts, models, languages, jurisdictions, and independent checks. Agreement is suspicious unless it comes from independent paths.

Node reputation

Nodes earn trust from uptime, correct answers, validated storage chunks, and positive contribution to ensemble quality. Bad nodes are sandboxed, audited, or excluded.

Pheromone encryption

Nodes share encrypted, content-blind traces about what worked, where quality emerged, and which routes are risky without exposing prompts, files, or raw answers.

Falsehood detection

Negative cache stores known hallucination regions and dead-end reasoning patterns, forcing heterogeneous cross-checks before weak consensus becomes accepted knowledge.

Economics

Free for contributors. Paid for full private mode and guarantees.

The free tier is not a giveaway; it is the supply side. People who contribute bandwidth, inference, or storage get access for free. Users who want full privacy, no contribution, dedicated capacity, or enterprise SLA pay.

Why now

The end of Big Tech and telecom default ownership.

I grew up in Poland in the 1990s without the electronics other kids had. Neighbors had Pegasus game consoles; friends had hi-fi towers; I did not. When I finally got my own tower, I almost slept next to it. At seven, I saw arcade machines at the Baltic Sea for the first time. Hardware felt rare, expensive, and magical.

Later a computer joined the house and I started modding connections between systems. That is the origin of BGML.ai: not ideology, but refusal to waste compute people already own. The protocol turns idle hardware into local-first AI, storage, backup, reputation, and shared knowledge.
